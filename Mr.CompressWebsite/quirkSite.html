⁂※Mr.Compress - Atsuhiro Sako※⁂

Quirk Explanation

Quick explanation: This quirk grants its user to shrink down anything inside a spherical area into a small marble
without actually damaging it. Compress will affect anything that is found within a specific
range of space around the intended target, including, for example, fire or pieces of the ground.

Abilities: This quirk is a very straight forward quirk with no super moves.
It can dig trough solid objects by compressing a specific area from the solid object.
The user can compress themselves into a marble in a spherical area,
as well as coming out of it again at free will.
When the user touches the target it wants to compress, it gets compressed into
a small marble,no matter how heavy it was before, it becomes the weight of a simple marble.

Weakness: The quirk relies entirely within the users arms, meaning if the user would loose both of their arms ,
the user would loose this quirk.
It is not possible that the quirk would spread to any objects nearby, meaning that the user cant
just touch the ground and all the things touching the ground would also be compressed, which puts
the user at a huge disadvantage when it comes to long-range fights.
The user can not compress very large objects, the limit being probatly just roughly bigger than
a person, since Mr.Compress never showed to compress something bigger than that.
